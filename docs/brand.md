# Cherie Amour Craftz Brand Guide

## Business Name

Cherie Amour Craftz

## Tagline

Custom crochet pieces made with love, made to fit you.

## Brand Mission

Cherie Amour Craftz creates handmade crochet pieces that celebrate individuality by combining craftsmanship with personalized fit, allowing every customer to own something made just for them.

## Brand Personality

- Handmade
- Elegant
- Feminine
- Warm
- Welcoming
- Personalized
- Beach-inspired
- Soft
- Premium without feeling luxury

## Target Audience

- Customers looking for custom crochet clothing and accessories
- People shopping for vacation, festival, swimwear, and resort pieces
- Customers who want personalized sizing
- Handmade gift shoppers

## Design Direction

- Soft white backgrounds
- Black typography
- Pastel coral, peach, yellow, and blush accents
- Rounded corners
- Airy spacing
- Large product imagery
- Minimal clutter
- Mobile-first layout

## Typography

### Headings

Preferred:

- DM Sans
- Poppins

### Body

Preferred:

- Inter

### Accent

Use sparingly:

- Playfair Display

Example use:

> Handmade with Love

## Buttons

### Primary Button

- Background: `#F48B82`
- Hover: `#E67870`
- Text: `#FFFFFF`
- Shape: rounded

### Secondary Button

- Background: `#FFFFFF`
- Border: `#F48B82`
- Text: `#1F1F1F`
- Hover: `#FFF4F2`
- Shape: rounded

## Component Style

### Cards

- White background
- Rounded corners
- Soft border
- Light shadow
- Spacious padding

### Forms

- Clear labels
- Large inputs
- Rounded corners
- Friendly validation messages
- Plenty of spacing

### Product Images

- Bright natural lighting
- White or soft neutral backgrounds
- Lifestyle photos encouraged
- Close-up texture shots encouraged
- Consistent image ratios

## Voice and Copy

The brand voice should feel warm, personal, and polished.

Examples:

- "Add to Bag" instead of "Add to Cart"
- "Complete Your Order" instead of "Checkout"
- "Let's Create Something Beautiful" for custom orders
